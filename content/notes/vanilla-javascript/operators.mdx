
## types of operators in Javascript 

  * === - equaility 
  * !== - not equal 
  * <   - less than 
  * >   - greater than 
  * <=  - less than or equal to 
  * >=  - greater than or equal to 
  * !   - Not operator 
  * +=  - Append 
  

  * +  - Add 
  * *  - multiply 
  * -  - subtract 
  * /  - divide 
  * ++ - increment 
  * --   decrement
